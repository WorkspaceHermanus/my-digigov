A trust model

How Trusted Credentials Work

Every credential is verifiable, portable and owned by the employee - built on open digital public infrastructure.

01

Verify

Departments issue a verified employee credential once records match.

02

Present

Public servants present proof when accessing workplace services.

03

Match

Systems check permissions and context without exposing extra data.

04

Complete

Tasks finish with a clear audit trail and a trusted status update.
